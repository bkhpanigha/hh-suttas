Hillside Hermitage Advanced Suttas Search


1. Choose your language: Select the language you'd like to search in. Keep in mind that some Pali terms might appear in English translations and comments.

2. Pick your books: Select the specific books you want to search within.

3. Refine your search: Decide if you want to find exact matches for your search terms. For example, if you search for "training", you'll only see results that contain that exact word, not words like "restraining".

4. Single Results: Choose whether you want to see only the first match for a given sutta.

5. Find what you are looking for: Type your search terms and press Enter.
